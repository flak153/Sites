Karen

Backstory:

Single mother with a garden that needs to be maintained in a drought prone area

Motivations:

Wants to grow vegetables in back yard in order to provide healthy food for children

Wants system to be completely independent of public utilities

Frustrations:

The entire set-up is quite pricey

Precise control over the watering algorithm requires programming experience

Ideal Experience:

Looks up correct settings for the vegetables she wants to keep online from other consumers and then forgets about it for 20 years
